Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely within your browser using modern web technologies. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring 100% privacy.
What image formats are supported?
We support all common web image formats including JPG, PNG, and WebP. The tool works best with high-resolution images.
Is it free to use?
Yes, it is completely free to use with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations on the number of images you can process.
